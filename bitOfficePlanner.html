Planner-Help

You can:

  1. Load/Save your data to its native format. This will save both the Contacts and Calendar in one file.
  2. Import/Export to CSV. This will import or export CSV from MS Outlook.

Note: the CSV is compatible with Outlook. When loading into Google Contacts or Calendar, import as if Outlook CSV. Outlook has more fields, so if they are present will be shown in the Notes or Details instead.
